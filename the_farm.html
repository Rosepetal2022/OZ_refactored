The Farm.

Oz Incorporated is situated on 45 acres just north of Canby, Oregon. It is thirty minutes from downtown Portland and is easily accessed via I-5 and I-205. Oz has been built from the ground up and is specifically designed to serve the exact demands of our clientele. This premiere facility has a 90' x 200', fully enclosed indoor arena and an impressive, all-weather, all season, 300' X 600' outdoor riding area, there is always ample space to ride. We have natural jumps that include banks, walls, and a devil's dyke.

Each of the 45 stalls is 12' x 12' with tamped dirt floors and rubber mats and has the availability of watering the horse with automatic water or by buckets. Five grooming stalls and two wash racks with heated water make for easy grooming. Twelve large grassed paddocks are a wonderful space for safe turn out and a riding trail starts through the trees and continues around the perimeter of the property. The locked tack room has cubbies for each client while the trunk room houses the show trunks.

The Clubroom, with its panoramic views and deck, is a perfect place for parents and friends to watch, listen to the lessons via the sound system, or wait in the heated or air-conditioned space. It is equipped with a full kitchen, cable tv and high speed wireless internet, making it easy to work or study and ride.
